About Us

DK Ventures is a trusted and progressive real estate company based in Pune, specializing in agriculture plotting, NA plotting, land development, industrial plotting, and land acquisition.

Established in 2006, DK Ventures has grown into one of the most reliable names in Pune’s real estate sector. With nearly two decades of experience and a strong foundation built on trust, transparency, and professionalism, we have successfully delivered over 60 lakh sq. ft. of residential plots and more than 150 acres of industrial land across Purandar, Daund, and nearby regions.

Our expertise lies in identifying high-potential locations, acquiring strategic lands, and developing them into well-planned, investment-worthy, and future-ready properties.

At DK Ventures, we believe real estate is not just about buying or selling land — it’s about creating opportunities, building security, and nurturing dreams.
