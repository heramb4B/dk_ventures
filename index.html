About DK Ventures

Transforming Land into Lifelong Value

At DK Ventures, we believe that every piece of land holds the potential to become someone’s dream. With strong expertise in agriculture plotting, NA plotting, land development, and land acquisition, we create well-planned, legally clear, and future-ready projects that reflect transparency, trust, and long-term value.

Headquartered in Pune, our operations focus on Purandar, Daund, and the western Pune regions— areas rapidly emerging as some of the most promising real estate destinations.

Each project is designed to offer the perfect balance of peaceful surroundings, excellent connectivity, and strong growth potential. Over the years, DK Ventures has built a reputation for delivering secure and appreciating developments that connect you closer to nature.

Whether you're looking for a serene weekend getaway, a farmland investment, or a residential NA plot, we help you find the right opportunity with complete legal clarity and personalized guidance.

Our Core Values

  • Client trust and satisfaction – building long-term relationships through transparency and reliability.
  • Exceptional customer experience – guiding clients smoothly through every step of the real estate process.
  • Market leadership and growth – expanding our reach while maintaining integrity and excellence.
